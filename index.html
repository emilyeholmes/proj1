Computer Science Intern at Los Alamos National Laboratory

Since May 2021, I've been interning in LANL's XCP-2 group, developing a Eulerian hydrocode called PAGOSA. Through contributions to their photon doppler velocimetry (PDV) capabilities, I was able to gain experience developing large-scale code and responding to user feedback to improve the software. I also learned about Fortran and Linux/UNIX. My project and presentation were awarded the Outstanding Student Presentation Award at the 2021 Student Symposium.
